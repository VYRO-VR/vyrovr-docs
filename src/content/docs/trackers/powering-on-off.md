---
title: Powering On & Off
description: Turn IBIS trackers on, off, and understand auto-sleep behavior.
---

## Power on

Press the button **once**. The LED turns on. The tracker starts broadcasting and, if it's already paired (all IBIS trackers ship pre-paired), it'll appear in your SlimeVR Server within a few seconds.

## Power off

**Hold the button** until the LED turns off. The tracker is now fully off and won't drain battery.

## Auto-sleep

If a tracker hasn't moved for **about 15 minutes**, it powers itself down automatically to save battery. To wake it, press the button once. This is also why a tracker you left "on" overnight will still have battery left in the morning.

## What if a tracker doesn't turn on?

1. Make sure it isn't completely dead — drop it on the charger for 10 minutes and try again.
2. Check the USB-C port for debris if charging doesn't engage.
3. If the LED won't light at all after a charge, see [Charging Issues](/troubleshooting/charging-issues/).

## "Reset" vs power

A single press while a tracker is **on** sends a **reset** signal (re-zeros rotation, same as a full reset triggered from the SlimeVR window). It does **not** power the tracker off. If you meant to power off, hold the button instead.

## Powering on the whole set quickly

When you're sitting down to play:

1. Pop the trackers off the charging dock
2. Press each button once in order around your body (left foot → right foot → left ankle → right ankle → left knee → right knee → hip → chest → etc.) — this makes it easier to match physical trackers to the body-part assignments in the SlimeVR window
3. Strap them on
4. Stand, do a full reset, play
