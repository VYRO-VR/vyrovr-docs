## Product lines

- **IBIS Core** — 6 trackers, full lower-body tracking
- **IBIS Advanced** — 8 trackers, lower body + elbows
- **IBIS Full Body** — 10 trackers, lower body + arms + chest

All IBIS trackers are SlimeVR-compatible and use the nRF 2.4 GHz wireless protocol via a USB receiver. They work alongside any standard SlimeVR Wi-Fi tracker and any Vive/Tundra tracker setup.

## About this site

The pages on docs.vyrovr.com are **AI-generated** from publicly available SlimeVR documentation (primarily [docs.slimevr.dev](https://docs.slimevr.dev)) and VYRO VR product information from [vyrovr.com](https://vyrovr.com), then reviewed and edited by the VYRO VR team. Some details may still be inaccurate or out of date.

If you spot a discrepancy, **please open an issue or pull request** on the source repository: [github.com/VYRO-VR/vyrovr-docs](https://github.com/VYRO-VR/vyrovr-docs). Every page has an **Edit this page** link in the footer — corrections are welcome.
