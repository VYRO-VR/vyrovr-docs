The **Arm Tracker Upgrade Kit** adds two trackers and two straps for upper-arm tracking. Combined with a Core (6) or Advanced (8) IBIS set, it gives you the same coverage as Full Body (10).

## What's in the kit

- 2 × IBIS trackers
- 2 × comfort straps (same design as your existing straps)

## Pair the new trackers

The kit ships pre-paired to your existing receiver as long as you ordered it together with your set. If you bought it later, you may need to pair manually — see [Pairing](/trackers/pairing/).

## Where they go

Upper arms. The strap sits on the **belly of the bicep**, not the shoulder. Tighten until snug — biceps shift more than calves when you flex, so this strap can be a touch firmer than your leg straps.

For the recommended orientation, see [Wearing Trackers](/straps/wearing-trackers/#upper-arm).

## Why add arm trackers?

Arms are the second biggest tracking improvement after lower-body (lower body alone covers feet, knees, and hips). Arm trackers add elbow position, which makes:

- Stretching, dancing, and air-drumming feel natural
- VRChat emotes that use elbow position work properly
- Avatar gestures look like your real movements

## When to skip them

If you're only doing standing rhythm games or seated experiences, arm trackers add little. Save your money for foot trackers or a premium strap upgrade instead.
