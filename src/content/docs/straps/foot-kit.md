The **Foot Tracker Upgrade Kit** adds two trackers and two straps dedicated to foot tracking. Foot trackers go on top of the foot (or sometimes the lower shin, depending on preference) and give you per-foot orientation in VR.

## What's in the kit

- 2 × IBIS trackers
- 2 × comfort straps

## Pair the new trackers

If purchased with your set, they'll be pre-paired. Otherwise, see [Pairing](/trackers/pairing/).

## Where they go

There are two common mounting positions:

- **Top of the foot** (most common) — strap across the instep, tracker on the bridge of the foot. Best for clear foot-pointing in VR.
- **Lower shin / above the ankle** — for users who don't like things on top of their foot. Less precise foot orientation, but still better than ankle-only.

See [Wearing Trackers](/straps/wearing-trackers/#feet) for the recommended orientation.

## Notes

- Make sure the tracker is **inside the silicone tray** before stepping into shoes/slippers. The tracker is small but it's not crush-rated.
- Foot trackers are the hardest for the receiver to see (low to the ground, often shadowed by your body). If you see dropouts, raise your receiver — see [Range & Placement](/receiver/range-and-placement/).
