The **Foot Tracker Upgrade Kit** adds two trackers and two straps dedicated to foot tracking. Foot trackers go on top of the foot and give you per-foot orientation in VR.

## What's in the kit

- 2 × IBIS trackers
- 2 × basic 30 mm silicone-backed straps

The Foot Tracker Upgrade Kit ships with **basic straps only**, whether or not your order includes a receiver. It does not include comfort/premium straps.

## Pair the new trackers

If purchased with your set, they'll be pre-paired. Otherwise, see [Pairing](/trackers/pairing/).

## Where they go

**Top of the foot only.** Strap across the instep, tracker on the bridge of the foot. Per [SlimeVR's mounting guidance](https://docs.slimevr.dev/server/putting-on-trackers.html), the tracker's "up" face should point toward the ankle and its "front" face should point toward the ceiling.

Do **not** mount foot trackers on the lower shin or ankle — the SlimeVR Server expects foot trackers on the foot itself, and tracking accuracy suffers if they're placed elsewhere.

See [Wearing Trackers](/straps/wearing-trackers/#feet) for the recommended orientation.

## Notes

- Make sure the tracker is **inside the silicone tray** before stepping into shoes/slippers. The tracker is small but it's not crush-rated.
- Foot trackers are the hardest for the receiver to see (low to the ground, often shadowed by your body). If you see dropouts, raise your receiver — see [Range & Placement](/receiver/range-and-placement/).
