---
title: Premium Strap
description: The premium strap is the same product as the comfort strap.
---

**Premium strap** and **comfort strap** are two names for the same product. Premium is the marketing name when it's bundled with the Premium set or sold separately as the **Premium Strap Bundle**; comfort is the name used elsewhere in these docs.

For assembly, wear, and care, see **[Comfort / Premium Strap](/straps/comfort-strap/)**.

Non-premium IBIS sets ship with **basic 30 mm silicone-backed straps** and a **basic chest harness** instead — see [What You Got](/getting-started/what-you-got/).
