Before you start, lay everything out on a flat surface and check it against the list for the kit you ordered. If anything is missing or damaged, reach out via [support](/support/) before you start charging or pairing.

## Common to every set

Every IBIS set ships with:

- **Basic straps** — 30 mm wide, silicone-backed straps (one per tracker)
- **Basic chest harness** — the chest strap that holds the chest tracker
- **USB receiver dongle**
- **USB extension cable**
- **USB-C charging dock**
- **USB-C charging cable**
- Spare parts: clips, screws (vary by run)

## IBIS Core (6 trackers)

- 6 × IBIS trackers
- Plus the items above

## IBIS Advanced (8 trackers)

- 8 × IBIS trackers
- Plus the items above

## IBIS Full Body (10 trackers)

- 10 × IBIS trackers
- Plus the items above

## Premium set

In addition to the items above, the Premium set includes **comfort straps** (also marketed as **premium straps** — same product, two names) in place of the basic straps. See [Comfort / Premium Strap](/straps/comfort-strap/).

## Upgrade kits (sold separately)

If you added an upgrade kit to your order, it ships in its own bag:

- **Arm Tracker Upgrade Kit** — 2 trackers + 2 basic straps for adding upper arms
- **Foot Tracker Upgrade Kit** — 2 trackers + 2 basic straps for feet
- **Premium Strap Bundle** — comfort/premium straps that replace your basic straps

## A note on charge state

Trackers ship charged, but they may have woken up during transit. It's normal for one or two to need a quick top-up before first use. See [Charging](/charging-and-battery/charging/).
