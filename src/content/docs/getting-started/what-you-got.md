Before you start, lay everything out on a flat surface and check it against the list for the kit you ordered. If anything is missing or damaged, reach out via [support](/support/) before you start charging or pairing.

## IBIS Core (6 trackers)

- 6 × IBIS trackers
- 6 × comfort straps with mounting trays and clips
- 1 × chest harness
- 1 × USB receiver dongle
- 1 × USB extension cable
- 1 × USB-C 10-port charging dock
- 1 × USB-C charging cable
- Spare parts: clips, screws (vary by run)

## IBIS Advanced (8 trackers)

Everything in Core, plus:

- 2 × additional IBIS trackers
- 2 × additional comfort straps (for elbows)

## IBIS Full Body (10 trackers)

Everything in Advanced, plus:

- 2 × additional IBIS trackers
- 2 × additional comfort straps (for upper arms)

## Upgrade kits (sold separately)

If you added an upgrade kit to your order, it ships in its own bag:

- **Arm Tracker Upgrade Kit** — 2 trackers + 2 straps for adding upper arms
- **Foot Tracker Upgrade Kit** — 2 trackers + 2 straps for feet
- **Premium Strap Bundle** — premium-grade straps that replace your comfort straps

## A note on charge state

Trackers ship charged, but they may have woken up during transit. It's normal for one or two to need a quick top-up before first use. See [Charging](/charging-and-battery/charging/).
