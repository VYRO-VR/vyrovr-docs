## The charging dock

Your kit includes a **USB-C 10-port charging dock**. It powers up to 10 trackers at once and keeps them organized between sessions.

### How to use it

1. **Drop each tracker into a port**, USB-C end down. The dock's port engages with the tracker's USB-C automatically.
2. **Plug the dock into any 5V USB source** — a PC USB port, a USB hub, or a 5V wall charger all work. Each tracker is current-limited to ~50 mA, so charge time is the same regardless of which 5V source you use.
3. **Wait until each tracker's charging LED turns off** (varies by firmware; some show steady, some pulse).

A full charge from empty takes a few hours. From half-full, much less.

## Any 5V USB source works

The dock and trackers don't fast-charge — each tracker draws only about **50 mA** during charging, well within what any standard 5V USB port can deliver. A PC USB port, a powered USB hub, and a 5V wall charger all charge at the same rate.

:::caution
**Do not** use chargers above 5V (no 9V/12V fast chargers). The trackers expect a standard 5V USB profile.
:::

## Charging a single tracker

If you don't want to set up the dock, you can charge any individual tracker with a USB-C cable into a 5V source. Same rules apply — 5V, no fast-charge profiles.

## How often to charge

50+ hours of battery means most users charge once every few sessions. A weekly top-up is a good default.

Trackers auto-sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity, so leaving them strapped to your body during a quick bathroom break doesn't drain them.

## When trackers are "fully charged"

Pull them off the dock and put them somewhere dry until your next session. **Don't leave them on the dock indefinitely once charged** — for battery longevity, see [Battery Care](/charging-and-battery/battery-care/).

## Charging problems?

- No LED activity when docked → see [Charging Issues](/troubleshooting/charging-issues/)
- One tracker won't charge → swap to a different dock port to rule out a port issue
- Tracker shows "charging" but never reaches full → very rare; raise it in [Discord](https://discord.gg/vyrovr) or [support](/support/)
