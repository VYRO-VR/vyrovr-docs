## Discord — fastest help

The [VYRO VR Discord](https://discord.gg/vyrovr) is the best place for setup help, troubleshooting, and questions that aren't yet covered on this site. The community is active and the team checks in regularly.

When asking for help, please share:

1. Which kit you have (Core / Advanced / Full Body)
2. What you're trying to do
3. What you've already tried
4. A short video or photo of the symptom if relevant

## Email

- **General / sales:** [sales@vyrovr.com](mailto:sales@vyrovr.com)
- **Other:** [vyrovr@gmail.com](mailto:vyrovr@gmail.com)

Email is right for **order issues, warranty/RMA, and slow-burn questions where Discord isn't the right place.** For live troubleshooting, Discord is faster.

## Warranty / Returns

See VYRO VR's [Refund Policy](https://vyrovr.com/policies/refund-policy) and [Terms of Service](https://vyrovr.com/policies/terms-of-service) for the formal version. For a defective tracker, receiver, or dock — email **sales@vyrovr.com** with your order number and a description of the fault.

## Where this documentation lives

The source for this site is on GitHub at [VYRO-VR/vyrovr-docs](https://github.com/VYRO-VR/vyrovr-docs). Every page has an **Edit this page** link in the footer — corrections and improvements are welcome via PR.

### About this content

These pages are **AI-generated** from publicly available SlimeVR documentation ([docs.slimevr.dev](https://docs.slimevr.dev)) and VYRO VR product information from [vyrovr.com](https://vyrovr.com), then reviewed and edited by the VYRO VR team. Some details may still be inaccurate or out of date. **If you spot a discrepancy, please open an issue or pull request** on the [repository](https://github.com/VYRO-VR/vyrovr-docs) — that's the fastest way to get it fixed for everyone.
